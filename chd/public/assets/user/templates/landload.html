How to access your Landlord's Account

This service is currently available for landlords using Managed or Rent Collection Services.

  • 1) We will send you a letter with your account log in details
  • 2) Visit Romans.co.uk and click on the account area
  • 3) Click Landlord's Account and then log in
  • 4) Change your password to something memorable
  • 5) You're now set up! You can access all of your essential information at the click of a button
